1. INUYASHA

A teenage girl named Kagome travels back in time to feudal Japan to aid Inuyasha, a young “half-demon”, in recovering the shards of a sacred jewel.

2. ATTACK ON TITAN

Next we have “Attack on Titan.”

This anime is known as Shingeki no Kyojin in Japanese and is based around giant humanoid creatures called TITANS that seek to enter their fortresses and devour all humans in sight. The anime starts with a colossal titan kicking a hole big enough for other titans to enter, and that shatters the piece and leads to lots of death and destruction.


If you'd like to watch the anime, I recommend checking out this link Here for more “Attack on Titan.”



3. ATTACK ON TITAN JUNIOR HIGH

This anime is a more kid-friendly version of “Attack on Titan”. It's a period of the original series where the characters are now attending junior high school. There only threat now from the Titans is them stealing their lunch.

4. ONE PIECE!

One Piece is a manga series created by Eiichro Oda that has been beloved by fans around the world since 1997. The anime follows an 18-year-old boy called Monkey D. Luffy who seeks to become “the king of the pirates” after finding the pirate Gold Roger’s treasure. Along the way, he makes friends who join his crew, such as Roronoa Zero, an infamous pirate hunter turned pirate who wants to become the world's greatest swordsman; Nami, who was once a thief and pirate hater turned the ship’s navigator; Usopp, a marksman; Sanji, the cook; Chopper, the ships doctor; and Nico Robin, to name a few.


If you'd like to watch the anime, I recommend checking out this link Here for more “One Piece!”



5. BLEACH

Ichigo Kurosaki is not your ordinary high school student. Why? Because he can see ghosts, and because of this, he has an encounter with a Hollow (an evil, hungry, tormented spirit) and is saved by Rukia Kuchiki, who is a Shinigami (death god).Shinigamis send good spirits to the Soul Society, which is the anime’s afterlife, while purifying Hollows so they may also go to the Soul Society. During the fight, Rukia gets injured and has to transfer her powers to Ichigo, which starts the chain of events of Ichigo learning who he is and saving the living and the dead.


If you'd like to watch the anime, I recommend checking out this link Here for more “Bleach.”



6. TOKYO GHOUL

This anime tells the story of Keneki Ken, a university student whose life turns upside down when his crush attacks and almost kills him since she turns out to be a Ghoul, a race of supernatural creatures that survive on human flesh. Her attempt at killing and eating him proved unsuccessful when heavy materials fell and crushed her, saving Kaneki, but he was already gravely wounded. To save Kaneki, the doctors gave him the woman’s organs. And in a surprising turn of events, the transplant transformed him into a “ half-ghoul.” Now, he must adjust and learn to live with his new chance at life.
